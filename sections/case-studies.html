OUR MISSION

The right help at the right time.

Startups and small businesses often have great ideas and reachable goals. However, without a good strategy, branding and financial management these will struggle to reach them. Our mission consists on being the help and tools they need, right when they need them.

Feel at home!

Inside Barcelona

Inside Barcelona is a company focused on short-term tourist apartment rentals. Its founders are two passionate travelers who want to present visitors with an experience that is not conventional. Our focus was to have the communications materials reflect the warmth of the company’s founders. We ask the clients themselves for permission to use photographs of their memories to create an authentic version of Barcelona.

Spreading Cryptos

NXTER

Nxter Magazine promote Nxt-based blockchain solutions – Nxt, Ignis, Ardor, child chains – and they develop software on the blockchain platform, as they believe it’s one of the most future-proof blockchain solutions ever created. Nxter needed a new brand that would allow sub-brands. Our work focused on drawing a new strategy away from the iconography related to the cryptocurrencies to focus on more human values.

Bringing life to life

Khuab

Khuab is an oncology clinic based on natural therapies. Its objective is to treat clients through an integrative medicine. At the state level, they are leaders in the sector for their non-invasive surgeries. Our goal was to create an identity that will reflect the organic way in which the institution deals with diseases. We use the seaweed as a metaphor for a new opportunity since they were the first living beings.
